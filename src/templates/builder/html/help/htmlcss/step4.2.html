Because the content assets are missing, the page shows {bakery.intro}, {bakery.featured} and {bakery.news} just the same way we wrote them down. Now let's create these content assets! Therefore, first click in the personal navigation area at "Static Content". The content assets are all hierarchically organized in sections and sub sections. Right now there is only one top section: "pages", which is empty.

Our content assets all began with bakery. This is the top section's name we want to expect it from. So we need to create that top section. Click at "Add" > "Add Top Section" and enter bakery. Then right-click "bakery" and click at "Add text". The first time, a content asset is created, Prails will ask for the language these content assets should be in first. Simply enter English and en_us, then press "Save".
