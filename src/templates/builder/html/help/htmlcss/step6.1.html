Let's create the about page. Therefore, first go back to the static content section in the personal navigation area. There, right-click at "pages" and choose "Add Text". As identifier, we simply enter about. For "Decorator", choose our newly created decorator Bakery:decorate.

As page title we choose to use About My Bakery. The all content we put in the Word-like field is our about page's content. We simply generate some nice text over at BlindTextGenerator.com and paste it as content. Mark something as a nice headline and save it.
