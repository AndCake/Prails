Images on a web page are an essential part of many designs. Be it logos, product images or tiny arrows in front of navigation items. In Prails, images are one kind of a resource that should be uploaded to Prails in order to use it. This is exactly what we're going to do now. Right now our page looks a little plain. We want to add a simple logo just in front of our headline.

You can download the logo right here.After downloading it, you may want to use it.

What we need to do for this, is first, upload a picture. Therefore, open the list of Modules, as you have learnt in the website basics and right-click "Bakery". A menu opens up, where you choose Edit Resources. Then click the Bulk Add button in order to upload the logo image. Click the respective upload button and select the cupcake file. It will upload and be stored as a new resource. After that is done, a new entry will appear in the list of resources. Double-click it in order to open it.
